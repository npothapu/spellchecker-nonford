BRAND & LOGO

Our soy checkoff brand is our messages, the look and feel of our marketing materials and how we each represent our soybean farmers across the country and all over the world. Every interaction performed on behalf of the soy checkoff continually shapes our audiences’ perceptions — which is why representing the brand consistently and properly in the marketplace is so vital.

BRAND GUIDELINES

Our brand guidelines govern the tone, composition, design and general look and feel of all soy checkoff branding. They are to be used by all who produce checkoff communications.
