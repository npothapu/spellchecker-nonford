ABOUT THE UNITED SOYBEAN BOARD

The United Soybean Board – your soy checkoff – helps create new opportunities for increased demand and a better bottom line for U.S. soybean farmers. Whether it’s production research, working with the supply chain or finding new markets for soy oil and meal both here and abroad, the soy checkoff has its farmers’ best interests at heart when investing their funds for maximum ROI.
