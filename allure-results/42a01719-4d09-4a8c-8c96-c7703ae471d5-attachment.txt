Who We Are

At VML, we prioritize building a connected community across more than 30,000 people in 150-plus offices around the world. By fostering understanding, belonging, and acceptance, we create an environment where employees can bring and be their authentic selves to work.

Our focus extends beyond just work; we value continuous learning, growth, and transformative experiences as essential elements of every individual's career at VML.

We deliver equitable outcomes and growth based on our four core values:

Connect Meaningfully

Foster purposeful relationships among colleagues and client partners.


Inspire Creatively

Encourage inventiveness and infuse creativity into every aspect of our offering.


Include Purposefully

Strive to ensure everyone feels included, valued and a true sense of belonging.


Approach Positively

Contribute optimism to every situation to discover the best way forward together.

OUR COMMITMENT TO EQUAL EMPLOYMENT OPPORTUNITY

At VML, we are committed to fostering an all-inclusive work environment that is both rewarding and career-forward. Our Inclusion, Equity & Belonging initiatives, alongside the VML Foundation, reflect our dedication to giving back and making a positive impact in our communities and beyond. Our people are the heartbeat of our organization—creators, doers, innovators, makers, and thinkers—who drive not just marketing, but meaningful experiences that resonate in every action and interaction.

VML is an equal opportunity employer and considers applicants for all positions without regard to race, color, religion or belief, sex, age, national origin, citizenship status, marital status, military/veteran status, genetic information, sexual orientation, gender identity, physical or mental disability, or other protected group status. We believe in creating a dynamic work environment that values diversity and inclusion and strives to recruit a diverse slate of candidates to help us achieve that goal.

To learn more about our Equal Opportunity [or EEO] hiring practices click here.

We will be happy to provide reasonable accommodation to individuals with disabilities who need an accommodation to use or access the VML career site or otherwise fully participate in the application process. Should you require an accommodation to assist in the completion of your application or other aspect of our recruitment process due to a disability, please call our Corporate HR department at +1 212 210 7000 or email accommodations@vml.com to request an accommodation. Please note that this contact is only for candidates with disabilities who are requesting accommodation. Emails sent for non-disability related issues, such as following up on an application, will not receive a response.

VML is aware of scams involving false offers of employment with our company. The false interviews and job offers use fake websites, email addresses, group chat and text messages. We never interview prospective candidates via instant message or group chat, nor do we require candidates to purchase products or services, or process payments on our behalf as a condition of any employment offer. For more information or if you have been targeted please reach out here.
